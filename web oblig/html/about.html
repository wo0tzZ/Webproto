About us, Probono

Pro-Bone-O celebrates animals and their owners. Our solutions is made with you, our costumer, in mind. Our supreme products will absolutely help you in your everyday life. Wether you are in need of easier ways to groom your pet, or simply, aquire a new companion; we got you covered.

Ever since the company was founded, in 2019, we have had a simple and clear statement saying:"We will strive to make innovative solutions that will improve animal welfare and the daily lives of animals, pets and their owners." To achieve this, our development teams are constantly gathering feedback from our regular costumers, pet communities and market research to make innovative products at a reasonable price.
